# Referral Program

Starting in [Season I](/season-1), the Duper Referral Program is in effect, and won't be affected by upcoming seasons.

Invite your friends to buy a season pass and you'll earn real-money rewards!

## How does it work?

- Each player has 1 unique referral code, unlimited use.
- You get 0.001 ETH for each friend who registers using your referral code and buys a season pass of any tier within 2 months of registration. Each referral counts only once.
