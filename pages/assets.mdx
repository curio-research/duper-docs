# Assets

Welcome to the Duper economy! Here's everything you need to know about the different assets in our ecosystem.

## üí≤ Dollars

Real U.S. dollars are used in game entry fees and prize pools.

You can top up or withdraw dollars from your account at any time. Note that withdrawals require a minimum of \$5 and are subject to a 7-day review period to prevent fraud.

## ‚ú® Sparks

Sparks are the primary currency in the Duper ecosystem. Use sparks to buy [Gold Keys](./lootboxes#how-to-open-a-lootbox) and extend your turn time. Buy sparks directly from the store, or earn them from season pass, quests, lootboxes, and limited-time events.

### Turn Time Extension

You can use sparks to extend your turn time up to 2 times per match.

- Casual (\$0.1 entry): 1st time 50 sparks, 2nd time 150 sparks
- Ranked (\$2 entry): 1st time 100 sparks, 2nd time 300 sparks
- Custom (at least \$2 entry): 1st time 100 sparks, 2nd time 300 sparks
- Duper League (\$10 entry): 1st time 200 sparks, 2nd time 600 sparks

### Emblem Points

Everyone starts with 1 emblem point which increments by 1 every round. Every time a player uses emblems, everyone else's emblem points increment by 1.

In casual mode, emblem points don't increment every round, only from another player using emblems.

### Emblem Fragments

Think of emblem fragments as one-time use emblems. Every emblem has emblem fragments, and they are available from lootboxes, season pass, and bundles.

## üéÅ Lootboxes

Lootboxes are live starting in Season IV! Get Emblems, skins, and other rare items you'd like by acquiring Keys to open Lootboxes.

### What's inside a lootbox?

Each lootbox opens to exactly 1 item. Below are all possible items from a lootbox:

- Song fragment (3 to combine into a song)
- Emblem fragment
- Cody emote
- \$2-voucher
- Emblem

Note: Emblems are the rarest item in a lootbox, and they can only be opened using gold keys.

### How to open a lootbox?

Opening lootboxes requires keys. There are two types of keys: gold keys and silver keys.

- Gold Key
  - Acquired from direct purchase and season pass benefits
    - Cost: 499 Sparks
  - 1 Gold Key opens 1 lootbox
- Silver Key
  - Acquired from daily quests and season pass benefits
  - Opening lootboxes using silver keys costs the following number of keys each week:
    - 1st lootbox: 2 silver keys
    - 2nd: 3 silver keys
    - 3rd: 4 silver keys
    - 4th and beyond: 5 silver keys
    - Cost resets every Monday
