## ðŸ—¿ Duper League

Duper League is a weekly tournament where players compete for a higher prize pool than regular Casual games, and where spectators get to bet on the outcome.

### Registration

- If you've made at least 5 Chips in Ranked / Casual payouts in the past week, you can sign up. Others will then be able to bet on you.
- Players must put up a 5-Chip stake when registering, which will be returned after participating in at least one of the two games.
- If you've played in Duper League in previous weeks but don't sign up for the current week, you will still appear on the leaderboard but others cannot bet on you.

### Game Format

- 2 games in a row, second game starting 1 hour after the first game starts
- Time: 1-3pm UTC every Sunday
- As player, you must show up within 5 minutes after each game starts. After 5 minutes, games will start and the matchmaking prioritizes matching players with different opponents.
- Entry fee: 10 Chips

### League Leaderboard

- Anyone who has played at least one Duper League match will show up on the leaderboard.
- Leaderboard is ordered by League game total profits.

### Season V Bonuses

- **Season V Participation Bonus**: All players who have completed 10+ Ranked or Casual games receive a 5-Chip voucher that expires at the end of the season

### Fees & Loss Protection

- Tournament fees are used to provide loss vouchers
- The two biggest losers of any league games have a 50% chance to receive a 5-Chip voucher that expires at the end of the season

### Betting

- Bet on who makes the most profit across this week's Ranked/Casual games times a multiplier + League games. Parimutuel odds.
  - Formula: Ranked/Casual game total profit this week \* 0.1 + League game total profit this week
  - The multiplier may change from week to week.
- Betting more than 50 Chips in a week will get you 3 Silver Key.
- Betting is cut off as soon as the first game starts.
- Competitors get 4% from all bets placed on them; Duper gets 1%.
- Betting rewards are settled after each week's League games.
