# XP and Rewards

## XP

XP is the bedrock of Duper's progression system.

### Importance of XP

Players with high XP will get privileges in the future, including but not limited to:

- Priority to participate in the first [Seasons](./seasons)
- Priority to launch their \<redacted\> and earn from speculation
- Priority to trade other players' \<redacted\> before others
- Exclusive roles in Discord

### How to Gain XP

You gain XP from playing games, having positive net payout from games, and generally contributing to the Duper ecosystem. Track your XP and XP gains on your profile page on Duper website.

## Rewards

There are massive rewards for playing Duper.

### $ARB Matching Rewards

We are partnered with Arbitrum to provide \$ARB matching rewards for players. Check out the official website for the specifics. The $ARB rewards are distributed to your wallet every week.
