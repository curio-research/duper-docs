# Duper Bowl

The Duper Bowl takes place from May 2nd to May 16th.

Anyone can participate by simply playing regular Ranked games with the "Qualifier" toggle.

Duper team puts up $5,000 in the final prize pool at the start of the tournament. The prize pool will further increase from matches and eliminations.

## Main Tournament Rules

- Phase 1: Qualifiers
  - Anyone can join the qualifiers by playing regular Ranked games. To join, simply toggle the "Qualifier" button in the lobby. You can switch between "Qualifier" and "Ranked" at any time.
  - When you have "Qualifier" toggle on, your payouts go into your tournament balance up to 100 chips before Phase II.
  - During the qualifiers and later phases, game fees from participants go into the final prize pool. Vouchers and loss raffle will be disabled.
- Phase 2: Playoffs
  - Anyone who has participated in the qualifiers can join the playoffs.
  - If you decide not to join the playoffs or quit during the playoffs, you can withdraw your entire tournament balance at any time, but 20% of it will roll into the final prize pool.
  - You can top up 100 Chips to your tournament balance anytime.
  - Schedule 
    - 2 matches a day for 2 days; 1-hour window for each match
    - Tentative window: 9-11pm SGT / 6-8am PT / 3-5pm CET
  - The entry fee for each match is fixed, and will be determined after qualifiers.
  - Match schedules are fixed by Duper team before games start, but not visible to players until after.
  - You must arrive in the first 10 minutes of each match window, or you will be eliminated, and your tournament balance will be rolled into the final prize pool.
  - You are also eliminated if your balance is less than the entry fee before Phase 2 ends.
  - Players with top 5 balances will advance to the finals.
- Phase 3: Finals
  - Finals are composed of 2 matches on a single day.
  - Each final match takes place with virtual entry fee of 1000 Chips.
  - Final prize pool split
    - 1st place: 60%
    - 2nd place: 24%
    - 3rd place: 16%
- During playoffs and finals, Ranked and Casual games will be disabled during the scheduled match times and resumed 1 hour after matches end on each day.
- Anyone caught to violate Community Rules will be immediately disqualified. Their tournament balance will be split 50-50 between the reporting player and the final prize pool.

## Spectating & Player Betting

- Two streamers will be selected to exclusively stream the Duper Bowl.
  - Gift: 10 5-Chip vouchers + 50% discount for next season's pass
  - Selected among creator ambassadors 3 weeks before Duper Bowl
- You can bet on the duper bowl winner with parimutuel style betting using chips.
  - Phase 2: Bet on who will advance to the finals
  - Phase 3: Bet on the winner of the finals
  - The rewards favor bets on players with fewer total bets placed on them. If a player with less total bets survives, each ETH bet on them will earn more than ETH bet on a player with more total bets. This creates an opportunity for higher returns betting on less popular players who end up surviving.
